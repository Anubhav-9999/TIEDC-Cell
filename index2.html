Who We Are

About JUIT TIEDC Cell

Fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among students

Empowering the Next Generation

TIEDC Cell ( Entrepreneurship Incubation and Development Cell) of JUIT fosters innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration among students. It empowers young minds to build, innovate, and lead real-world solutions.

We provide a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to transform their ideas into impactful ventures through mentorship, workshops, networking opportunities, and funding support.

Our mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem where creativity meets execution, helping students develop the skills and mindset needed to succeed in today's dynamic business landscape.
